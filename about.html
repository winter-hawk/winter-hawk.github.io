About


I'm currently a reporting intern at the Hyattsville Life and Times, a staff writer for The Campus Trainer and a copy-editor for Stories Beneath the Shell. I am also the Director of Communications for the Student Government Association. I attend the University of Maryland, College Park, where I plan to graduate from in Spring 2023.

During my internship, I report local issues and write 2-3 articles a month for the online and print publication. Throughout the semester, I also write biweekly articles for The Campus trainer and copy-edit roughly 2 storied a week for Stories Beneath the Shell.

I am pursuing a career in magazine publication and find my home in profiles and feature stories.
